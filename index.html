Our Story

BUL Armory is an Israeli firearms manufacturer,designing firearms
for defensive carry, duty carry, and practical shooting sports.
BUL was founded in 1990,It initially produced only a variant of the
1911 pistol (the BUL M-5). Later it began producing the BUL Storm and
BUL Cherokee series of pistols, both CZ 75 variants.
